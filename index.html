A Little About Me

Hey, my name is Kedrick Akons. I am currently a first-year graduate student at the University of Michigan School of Information focusing on User Experience Design/Human-Computer Interaction. I received my Bachelor of Arts degree in Political Science from the University of Michigan in May 2020.


I am a passionate designer who values consumer privacy and accessibility. I enjoy the technology field. In my free time, I watch YouTube videos to see what's going on in the field. A few of the tech channels that I subscribe to are Marques Brownlee, Brian Tong, and Rene Ritchie. I also enjoy listening to music. Some of my favorite genres are Hip-Hop/Rap, R&B/Soul, and Pop.
